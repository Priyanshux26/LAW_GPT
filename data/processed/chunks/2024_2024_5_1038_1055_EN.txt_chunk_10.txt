We may note that perhaps mere existence of a large campus area would not per se mean that the capacity has been enhanced or augmented. What is required to be seen is that whether the requisite facilities for each individual prisoner are adequate in terms of sleeping area, mobility within the prison, kitchen/food, health facilities, other matters etc. 41. Thus, States/UTs, in their proposed affidavits, should address all issues holistically, including inmate-capacity enhancement/ augmentation. Other logistics such as creation of posts of wardens/ cooks/doctors/various jail staff etc. should also be factored in. 42.
