While deciding any E ownership and title of the original writ petitioner in theland in question, the High Court was not justified in entertaining the writ petition. Under the circumstances also the impugned and passed by the High Court is unsustainable. 5. In view of the above and for the reason stated above present appeal succeeds. The impugned and passed by the High Court is hereby quashed and set aside.
