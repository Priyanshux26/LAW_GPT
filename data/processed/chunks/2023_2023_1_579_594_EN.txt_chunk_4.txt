At this stage it is required to be noted that before the DRT-I, it was the case on behalf of the borrowers that the property Item No.8 was the agricultural land and therefore, the same could not have been the subject matter of auction under the provisions of the SARFAESI Act. Therefore, the DRT- I held that the lands in question (property Item No.8) were not exempted from the provisions of the SARFAESI Act.
