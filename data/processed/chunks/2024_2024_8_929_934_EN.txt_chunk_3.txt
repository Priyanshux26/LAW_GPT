In view of the above evidence, we are of the opinion that the prosecution had succeeded in proving the guilt of the appellant beyond reasonable doubt. Even the two Courts below have also discussed the said evidence in detail and found him guilty of the charges levelled against him. 934 [2024] 8 S.C.R. Digital Supreme Court Reports 14.
