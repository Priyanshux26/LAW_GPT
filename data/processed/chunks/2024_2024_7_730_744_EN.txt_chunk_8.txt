Having bestowed our thoughtful consideration on these two s and after taking note of the fact that the entire process in the case in hand might take more than a year, it seems to us that fixation of monthly honorarium may not be desirable. Similarly, we do not want to leave it for the learned Chairperson or members of the HPSC to fix their own honorarium as it is likely to cause embarrassment to them. Considering all these aspects in view, we issue the following directions:- (i) The Chairperson of the HPSC shall be entitled to an honorarium of Rs. 2 lakhs per sitting day, when effective proceedings are held. This will be in addition to travelling, boarding and other miscellaneous expenses as may be incurred in discharging the assigned responsibilities; (ii) The learned Member, who is a former Judge of the High Court shall be entitled to an honorarium of Rs. 1.50 lakhs per sitting day, when effective proceedings are held.
