Miscellaneous Application No. 267 of 2024 in (Civil Appeal No. 3082 of 2022) 06 September 2024 [Vikram Nath* and Prasanna Bhalachandra Varale, JJ.] Issue for Consideration Miscellaneous application is filed by the appellant seeking direction to reinstate him into service as civil judge with all the consequential benefits in view of the dated 20.04.2022 passed by the Supreme Court in C.A.No. 3082 of 2022.
