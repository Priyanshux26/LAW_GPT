The Union Ministry of Information and Broadcasting1 revoked the permission which it had granted to Madhyamam Broadcasting Limited2 to uplink and downlink a news and current affairs television channel called “Media One”. The appellants, comprising of MBL, the trade union F of working journalists, and the editor, Senior Web Designer and Senior Cameraman of Planetcast Media Services Ltd, initiated proceedings under Article 226 of the Constitution before the High Court of Kerala for challenging the action of the first respondent. By a dated 8 February 2022, a Single Judge dismissed the petitions. The writ appeal G was dismissed by the Division Bench of the High Court by a dated 2 March 2022.
