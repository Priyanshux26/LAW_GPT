It appears that between April 2022 & June 2023, the Bank attempted eight auctions but all failed. 11. It appears that the borrowers informed the Bank that they were trying to sell the secured asset but were not getting good offers. The borrowers informed the Bank that the maximum they might be able to fetch from the sale of the secured asset would be around Rs. 91-92 crore and they were willing to settle the entire account by offering such amount to the Bank. 12. The Bank however decided to go for one more auction. On 14.06.2023, the Bank published the notice of sale in terms of Rule 8(6) of the SARFAESI Rules for the 9th time.
