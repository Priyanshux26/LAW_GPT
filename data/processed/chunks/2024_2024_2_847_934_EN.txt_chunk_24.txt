The Uttar Pradesh Municipalities Act, 1916 (hereinafter referred to as “Act of 1916”) consolidates and amends the law relating to Municipalities in the erstwhile United Provinces and presently State of Uttar Pradesh. The city of Lucknow was a municipality and later was constituted as Nagar Nigam or Corporation under the Act of 1959 and till then the Act of 1916 was applicable. Hence, the relevant provisions of the Act of 1916 are extracted as under: “128.
