There are several ways by which, a power may be conferred upon the Governor, or qua the Governor, which will enable him to exercise the said power by virtue of his offi ce as Governor. Therefore, there can be no gainsaying that all the powers that are exercisable by the Governor by virtue of his offi ce can be exercised only in accordance with the aid and advice of the Council of Ministers except insofar as the Constitution expressly, or perhaps by necessary implication, provides otherwise.
