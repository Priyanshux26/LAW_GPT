That the surplus funds are not used directly by the clubs towards any specifi c service, maintenance or any other direct benefi t for the members of the club, but are deposited in the form of fi xed deposits in banks to be at the disposal of the said banks for its operations vis-a-vis third parties, namely, customers of the banks.
