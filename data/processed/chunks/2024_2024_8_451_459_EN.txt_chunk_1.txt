The facts, in brief, giving rise to the present appeal are as given below: 3.1 On 27th January 2012, the appellant married one Ms. Subrata Das and out of the wedlock two children were born – the first child, Master Divyanshu Das, on 11th September 2013, and the second child, Ms./Baby Sugandha Das, on 20th April 2021. 3.2 When their daughter was only 10 days old, the appellant unfortunately lost his wife on 30th April 2021, due to Covid-19 infection.
