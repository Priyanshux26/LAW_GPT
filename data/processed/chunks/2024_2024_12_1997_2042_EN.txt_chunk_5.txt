[Paras 85, 88] Interpretation of Statutes – Taxing Statutes – Interpretation thereof: Held: Taxing statutes, being penal in nature, must be construed strictly – The power to levy a tax or fee cannot be inferred by implication but must be expressly conferred by Statute – Under our Constitutional framework, no private entity can be granted the authority to levy taxes or fees, for such powers are exclusively vested in public authorities.
