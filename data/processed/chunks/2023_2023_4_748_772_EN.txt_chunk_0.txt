G Special or fixed term sentence – Imposition of, as an alternative to death penalty in serious crimes – Held: Power to impose special or fixed term sentence beyond the minimum sentence specified in s. 433A CrPC reserved solely with High Courts and Supreme Court as per decision in *Sriharan’s case – Code of H Criminal Procedure – s.433A. 748 VIKAS CHAUDHARY v. Partly allowing the appeals, the Court HELD: 1.1 The appropriateness of imposing a punishment (termed as a special or fixed term sentence) in serious crimes, C which carried, as a sentencing option, the death penalty (apart from life sentence), was considered by this court in Sriharan’s case.
