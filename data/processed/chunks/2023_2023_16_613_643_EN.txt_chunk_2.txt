Out of the total eight vacancies, six were “existing vacancies” and two were “anticipated vacancies”. The preliminary examination for these posts was held on 12.05.2013 of which the results were declared on 15.06.2013. The candidates, who had qualifi ed preliminary examination participated in the main written examination which was held between 15th July, 2013 to 18th July, 2013. Eighty candidates qualifi ed in the written examination and were ultimately called for the interview, which was held on 07th and 08th October, 2013.
