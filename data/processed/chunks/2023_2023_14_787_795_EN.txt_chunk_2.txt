The accused pleaded not guilty and claimed to be tried. After hearing the arguments of both the parties, the Ld. However, the husband (accused no. 1), i.e. the appellant herein, was convicted for the off ence punishable under Section 498A and Section 306 IPC. The High Court of Karnataka, vide dated 7.11.2007, partly allowed the appeal. KAMALAKAR v. The sentence to undergo rigorous imprisonment for 2 years and to pay a fi ne of Rs. 2000/- for the off ence under Section 498A was however sustained. 5.
