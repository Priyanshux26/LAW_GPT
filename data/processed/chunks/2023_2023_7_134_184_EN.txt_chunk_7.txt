The High Court vide its dated 11.09.2014 rejected the Crl. O.P. No. 6371 of 2014, holding inter alia as under: (i) In the year 2013, the CBI came into possession of certain fresh materials warranting the re-opening of the investigation C against the accused persons. (ii) Under 173(8) of the CrPC, a police officer can carry on further investigation even after a report under Section 173(2) of the CrPC is submitted, in view of Section 173(8) of the CrPC and held in Vinay Tyagi v. Irshad Ali alias D Deepak and Others reported in (2013) 5 SCC 762, with the only rider being that the police should seek formal permission from the Court. (iii) Acceptance of the final report by the Magistrate does not debar him from taking cognizance if on further investigation, E fresh material comes to light.
