Facts giving rise to filing of Transferred Case (C) No.2 of 2023: 4. In pursuance of the directions issued vide first of NGT and in exercise of the powers conferred upon it under the TCP Act and the 1978 Rules framed thereunder, the State of Himachal Pradesh published a draft development plan on 8th February 2022. It is to be noted that various directions were also issued by the High Court of Himachal Pradesh from time to time in CWP No. 4595 of 2011 for finalization of the development plan in accordance with the TCP Act. The State of Himachal Pradesh also invited objections and suggestions from the general public in relation to the draft development plan. In all, 97 objections/suggestions were received by the State of Himachal Pradesh within stipulated time-period and the same were heard by the Director in due course. CWP Nos. 23 and 37 of 2022 were also filed before the High Court of Himachal Pradesh praying inter alia for stay of the draft development plan. 4.1 In the meantime, respondent No.1 herein-Yogendera Mohan Sengupta filed another OA (No. 297 of 2022) before the NGT in relation to the draft development plan. The NGT, vide interim dated 12th May 2022, stayed the draft development plan and restrained the State of Himachal Pradesh from taking any further steps in pursuance of the draft development plan.
