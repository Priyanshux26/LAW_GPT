(iii) If crop is standing on the acquired land or building/ structure exists, mere going on the spot by the authority concerned will, by itself, be not sufficient for taking possession. (iv) If the acquisition is of a large tract of land, it may not be possible for the acquiring/designated authority to take physical possession of each and every parcel of the land and it will be suffi cient that symbolic possession is taken by preparing appropriate document in the presence of independent witnesses and getting their signatures on such document.
