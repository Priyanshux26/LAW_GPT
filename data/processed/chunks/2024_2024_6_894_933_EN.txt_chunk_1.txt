[Paras 54-58, 46, 65, 67, 69] Review Jurisdiction – Scope: Held: Scope of review by this Court is very limited – Review would be permissible only if there is a mistake or error apparent on the face of the record or any other sufficient reason is made out – Review proceedings cannot be equated with the original hearing of the case – The review of the would be permissible only if a material error, manifest on the face of the , undermines its soundness or results in miscarriage of justice – Such an error should be an error apparent on the face of the record and should not be an error which has to be fished out and searched.
