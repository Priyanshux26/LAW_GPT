F The Impugned Judgement 5. The Impugned records the stand of the Respondents, placing reliance on various clauses of the 1999 agreement and the Terms and Conditions of Supply of Electricity to justify their stand as being entirely permissible. Having referred to the contents of the clauses, the G High Court held that it was not open for the Appellant to pay lesser charges than that of the contracted demand in absence of a sanction in respect thereof by the board.
