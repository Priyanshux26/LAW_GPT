In fact, Victory does not even have the status of a lessee, but is only a licensee. A license does not create any interest in the immovable property. D 50. Therefore, NCLT as well as NCLAT were right in holding that the possession of the Corporate Debtor, of the property needs to be protected. This is why a direction under Regulation 30 had been issued to the local district administration. E Conclusion 51. In the light of the above, we are of the considered view that the impugned s do not call for any interference. Hence, the appeals are dismissed. No costs.
