Applying the law laid down by this Court in the case of Indore Development Authority (supra) to the facts of the case on hand, the impugned and passed by the High Court declaring that D the acquisition with respect to the lands in question is deemed to have lapsed under Section 24(2) of the Act, 2013 is unsustainable and the same deserves to be quashed and set aside and is accordingly quashed and set aside. There shall not be any deemed lapsed as observed and held by the High Court. E Present appeal is accordingly allowed. However, in the facts and circumstances of the case, there shall be no as to costs. Pending application, if any, also stands disposed of. Divya Pandey Appeal allowed.
