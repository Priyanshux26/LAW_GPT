[Paras 20 (c) and 14] Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016 – When is the debt, an operational debt: Held: Where one party owes a debt to another and when the creditor is claiming under a written agreement/ arrangement providing for rendering ‘service’, the debt is an operational debt only if the claim subject matter of the debt has some connection or co-relation with the ‘service’ subject matter of the transaction.
