[Per Court] Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 – s.125 – Muslim Women (Protection of Rights on Divorce) Act, 1986 – s.127(3)(b) – If Section 125 of the CrPC is also resorted to by a divorced Muslim woman, as per the definition under the 1986 Act, whether any passed under the provisions of 1986 Act shall be taken into consideration under Section 127(3)(b) of the CrPC: Held: Yes – If Section 125 of the CrPC is also resorted to by a divorced Muslim woman, as per the definition under the 1986 Act, then any passed under the provisions of 1986 Act shall be taken into consideration under Section 127(3)(b) of the CrPC.
