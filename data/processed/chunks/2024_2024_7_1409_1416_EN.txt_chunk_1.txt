This dispute traces its origins to the alleged act of putting-up of a signboard by the respondent on the subject-premises,1 which according to the appellant, obstructed the hoarding put up by the appellant. Thus, the appellant/plaintiff filed C.S. The position of the parties was that the appellant was a tenant on the ground floor of the building in question, whereas the respondent was a tenant on the first floor. 3. Summons in the suit was served on the respondent, which entered appearance on 03.02.2000. It appears that the official website of the High Court showed the status of the suit as having been ‘disposed of’ on 01.03.2000.
