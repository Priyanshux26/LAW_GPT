Hence we set aside the findings of guilt and sentence arrived at vide dated C 28.08.2004 by the Ld. Trial Court as subsequently affirmed by the High Court in its judgement dated 21.07.2017 in CRA No.940 of 2004 titled as Bhainsa @ Nandlal and Anr. vs. The State of Chhattisgarh. 29. The appeal is allowed and the Appellant stands acquitted of all the charges framed against him. D We direct the Appellant Pradeep Kumar be released forthwith unless required in any other case.
