SHAH, J.] Under the circumstances to continue the criminal proceedings A against the appellants – accused arising out of the complaint and to face the trial by the accused as per the summoning is nothing but an abuse of process of law and court and this is a fit case to quash the entire criminal proceedings arising out of the complaint filed by the respondent No. 1 including the summoning passed by the learned B Trial Court. 7. In view of the above and for the reasons stated above, present appeals succeed. The impugned and passed by the High Court dismissing the revision application is hereby quashed and set aside. The passed by the Trial Court dated 04.11.2019 summoning C the appellants – accused to face the trial for the offences under Sections 420, 465, 466, 467, 468, 471 read with 120B IPC is hereby quashed and set aside. Present appeals are accordingly allowed.
