Matrimonial Cases – Transfer Petitions – Held: Number of transfer petitions are filed in matrimonial cases, primarily by the F wives seeking transfer of the matrimonial proceedings initiated by the husband – Normally, Supreme Court has been accepting the prayer made while showing leniency towards ladies – However, this leniency was being misused by women – Hence, each and every case has to be considered on its own merits.
