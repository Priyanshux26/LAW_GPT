Taking into account the stamp duty of Rs. 46,700/- paid by the appellants, they were directed to pay a deficit stamp duty of Rs.1,34,14,930/-. A penalty of Rs. 27,00,000/- was imposed on the appellants. Moreover, they were directed to pay interest at the rate of 1.5% per month on the deficit stamp duty from the date of the sale deed G till the realisation of the amount. 7.According to the case of the appellants, on 1st February 2012, they paid a stamp duty of Rs.70 lakhs by demand draft as coercive action was likely to be taken against them.
