RELEVANT FACTS 2. The Constitution (Application to Jammu and Kashmir) , 2019 bearing C.O. No.272 was issued by the Hon’ble President of India C on 5th August 2019. The said was issued in the exercise of powers conferred by clause (1) of Article 370 of the Constitution of India. The said directed that all the provisions of the Constitution, as amended from time to time, shall apply in relation to the State of Jammu and Kashmir, subject to modifications made to Article 367 as set out in the said . By the said , Clause (4) was added to Article 367 providing D that the expression “Constituent Assembly of the State referred to in clause (2)” in the proviso to clause (3) of Article 370 of the Constitution, shall be read as “Legislative Assembly of the State”. On 6th August 2019, a declaration under Clause (3) of Article 370 of the Constitution bearing C.O.No.273 was made by the Hon’ble President of India on the E recommendation of the Parliament, by which it was declared that all the clauses of Article 370 shall cease to be operative. 3. The Jammu and Kashmir Reorganisation Act, 2019 (for short, ‘the J&K Reorganisation Act’) was enacted which provided for the reorganisation of the State of Jammu and Kashmir by dividing it into two F Union Territories.
