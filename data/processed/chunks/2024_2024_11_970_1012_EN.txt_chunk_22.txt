The proviso to Rule 15(1) provides that the standard of accessibility with respect to other services and facilities shall be specified by the Central Government within a period of six months from the date of notification of the RPWD rules. Rule 15(2) stipulates that the concerned Ministries and departments shall ensure compliance with the standards of accessibility specified in the rule through the concerned domain regulators or otherwise. 63. As required by the proviso to Rule 15(1), subsequent to the notification of the RPWD Rules, additional standards have been specified by various ministries of the Central Government.
