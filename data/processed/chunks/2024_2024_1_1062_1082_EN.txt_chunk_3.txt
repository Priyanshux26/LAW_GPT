There is a society by the name of Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Mul1 registered under the Societies Registration Act, 18602 as a charitable society since 1946. The Society in its turn framed its rules and regulations. Later on, the Society was registered as a Public Trust under the Bombay Public Trusts Act, 19503. The rules and regulations of the Society were incorporated as its bye-laws and were duly registered under the Trusts Act. 3. As per the rules and regulations, the Society has four types of members i.e. Life members, Employee members, Ordinary members and Donor members. The members of each category were required to pay an annual membership subscription of Rs. 11/- per year to the Society. 4.
