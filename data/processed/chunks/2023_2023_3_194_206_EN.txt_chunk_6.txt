Now so far as the issue, “whether against the corrigendum to the EC along with additional conditions, an appeal before the NGT would be maintainable or not” is concerned, having heard learned counsel appearing on behalf of the respective parties, we are of the opinion that E an aggrieved person may always challenge the corrigendum to the EC, however, the appeal will be restricted to the corrigendum to the EC on additional conditions only, if the original EC is not under challenge and/or the original EC has been confirmed by the NGT earlier on certain conditions which have not been challenged.
