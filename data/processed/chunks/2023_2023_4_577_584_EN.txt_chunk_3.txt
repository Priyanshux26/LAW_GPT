We are of the view that in the case in hand, several important factors ought to have been kept in mind by the learned High Court while considering the prayer of the respondents for their enlargement on regular F bail. It was apparently a case of contract killing. There is material to indicate the motive behind the commission of gruesome murder of Ashfaque Ahmad. Both the respondents have chequered criminal record and it is difficult to accept G their sweeping statement that all the cases registered against them are politically motivated.
