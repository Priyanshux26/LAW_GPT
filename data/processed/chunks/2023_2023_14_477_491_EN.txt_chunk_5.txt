Section 19 provides the appellate procedure by allowing any person who is aggrieved by refusal of information to seek an eff ective redress and remedy. Section 19(1) allows any person who does not receive a decision within the time specifi ed in Section 7 to prefer a fi rst appeal to a senior offi cer of CPIO or SPIO. Section 7(3) allows any person who is aggrieved by the decision of such senior offi cer of CPIO or SPIO to fi le a second appeal with the CIC or SIC. In such proceedings, the onus to prove that the denial of request was justifi ed lies on the CPIO or SPIO who denied the request.
