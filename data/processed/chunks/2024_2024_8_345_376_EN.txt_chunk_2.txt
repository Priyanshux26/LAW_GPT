[Paras 41, 45] Constitution of India – Article 136 – Concurrent findings by courts below – Exercise of jurisdiction u/Article 136: Held: While exercising jurisdiction under Article 136 this Court will not interfere with the concurrent findings recorded by the courts below – However, where the evidence has not been properly appreciated, material aspects have been ignored and the findings are perverse, this Court would certainly interfere with the findings of the courts below though concurrent.
