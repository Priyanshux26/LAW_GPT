For area under open belt admeasuring 108501.12 sq. ft. enhanced compensation at Rs. 50 per sq. ft. was awarded. For the balance area of divided plots admeasuring 28809.84 sq. ft., Rs. 25/- per sq. ft. was awarded. [2024] 7 S.C.R. 2289 Kazi Akiloddin v. State of Maharashtra & Ors. 100.
