The quantum of family pension must be increased as per the same multiplier/index of rationalization applicable for pension. This is because the same C factors which are applicable to pay and pension leading to their increase also equally apply to family pension. The Commission has also recommended the same. The last recommendation is that on the income limit prescribed by States to be eligible for family pension. The minimum limit prescribed by the Commission was Rs. 30,000/-.
