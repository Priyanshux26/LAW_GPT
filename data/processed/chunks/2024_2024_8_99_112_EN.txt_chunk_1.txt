Digital Supreme Court Reports / of the Supreme Court Vikram Nath, J. WRIT PETITION (CIVIL) NO. 110 OF 2006: 1. This writ petition has been filed under Article 32 of the Constitution of India by the petitioner-People’s Rights and Social Research Centre, a Delhi-based non-governmental organization, seeking intervention of this Court in addressing the grave issue of “Silicosis” among workers in various industries across the country. The petitioner organization, registered under the Societies Registration Act since December 20, 1999, has been actively involved in occupational health work, specifically concerning stone crusher workers, stone quarry workers and construction workers. 2. Silicosis is an incurable occupational lung disease caused by prolonged inhalation of silica dust and it has been rampant throughout India due to inadequate detection, monitoring, and remedial measures.
