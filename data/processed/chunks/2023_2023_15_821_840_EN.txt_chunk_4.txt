The Learned Single Judge in Civil Review, examined the Limitation Act, 1963 along with various decisions rendered by this Court and concluded, after detailed consideration that since an Appeal under Section 22 (8) has been provided to a Principal Court of Civil jurisdiction without being restricted by a period of Limitation it would be treated as an Appeal provided under the Code, that is the Code of Civil Procedure14, to a Court subordinate to that of a High Court and to such an Appeal, the period of Limitation as prescribed under Article 116 of the Limitation Act, 1963 would apply.
