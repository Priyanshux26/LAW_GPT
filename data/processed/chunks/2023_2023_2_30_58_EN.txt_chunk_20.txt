The said is punitive in nature and E was passed to short-circuit the disciplinary proceedings pending against the appellant and ensure his immediate removal. The impugned passed by the respondents does not pass muster as it fails to satisfy the underlying test of serving the interest of the public. 40. In view of the above discussion, it is deemed appropriate to F reverse the impugned dated 31st May, 2022 and quash and set aside the dated 27th September, 2019 passed by the respondents, compulsorily retiring the appellant.
