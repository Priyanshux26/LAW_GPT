At the outset, it would be appropriate to advert to the relevant facts having a material bearing on the lis. Facts 5. Respondent was awarded a contract work relating to construction of approaches to flyover at the level crossing on New Rohtak Road with clover-leaf slip road and service road etc. by the appellant. A contract agreement was entered into between the parties in this regard on 11.11.1983. The work however could not be completed even beyond the stipulated date of completion till March, 1990 and, therefore, the work had to be closed in an incomplete form. The main reason for closing the work was non-availability of site. 5.1. The last payment to the tune of Rs. 4,71,48,122.00 was made to the respondent on 09.02.1988.
