Case Arising From CIVIL APPELLATE JURISDICTION : Civil Appeal No.3854 of 2014 From the and dated 27.09.2012 of the High Court of Madras in SA No.1926 of 2004 Appearances for Parties Dama Seshadri Naidu, Sr. Adv., G. Balaji, Advs. for the Appellant. V. Ramasubramanian, Adv. for the Respondents. 330 [2024] 2 S.C.R. Digital Supreme Court Reports / of the Supreme Court Sanjay Karol, J. 1. The action that set in motion the instant dispute was in the year 1947, when a mother transferred property inherited at the death of her husband, in one form to her two sons and in another, to her daughter. Some forty-odd years later, the daughter’s husband filed a suit in respect of such property, in 1993. The Additional District Munsiff1 decided the matter in 1999. The Additional District and Session Judge2 returned a decision on the First Appeal in 2002. The Second Appeal was decided by the High Court3 in 2012.
