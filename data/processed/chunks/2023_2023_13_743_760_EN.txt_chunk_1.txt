THE CHALLENGE 1. This appeal, at the instance of certain affected landowners, challenges an dated 21st December 2011 passed by a learned Single Judge of the High Court of Delhi (“High Court”, hereafter). By the under challenge, the High Court allowed an application filed by the Union of India (“first respondent”, hereafter) under section 5 of the Limitation Act, 1963 (“Limitation Act”, hereafter) and thereby condoned the delay of around 479 days in presentation of an appeal 746 SUPREME COURT REPORTS [2023] 13 S.C.R. from the decision of the Reference Court under section 18 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894. THE ISSUE 2. The limited issue that we are tasked to decide is, whether the High Court was justifi ed in condoning the delay in presentation of the appeal. In the process, we need to necessarily consider whether the fi rst respondent had shown suffi cient cause for which the appeal could not be presented within the prescribed period of limitation.
