The impugned and passed by the High Court allowing the writ petition and declaring that the acquisition in respect of the land in question is deemed to have lapsed under Section 24(2) of the Act, 2013 is hereby quashed and set aside. The original writ D petition before the High Court stands dismissed. There shall be no deemed lapse of the acquisition with respect to the land in question under Section 24(2) of the Act, 2013. Present appeal is accordingly allowed. No costs. E Pending applications, if any, also stand disposed of. Ankit Gyan Appeal allowed. (Assisted by : Abhishek Agnihotri, LCRA) F G H
