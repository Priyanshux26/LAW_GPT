The respondent issued a Letter of Award for executing Capital Dredging Phase-III at Kamarajar Port to the appellant for an approximate sum of Rs 274 crores. Capital dredging of Coal Berth 3 & Coal Berth 4 and their approaches; c. Removal of onshore boulders and transportation to the designated area; d. Removal of offshore boulders and transportation to the designated area; e. Removal of offshore identified debris/wrecks; and f. Environmental monitoring. 4.
