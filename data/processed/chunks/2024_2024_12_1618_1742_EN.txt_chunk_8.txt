[Paras 126, 127 and 154(iii)] Maxims – Expressio Unius Est Exclusio Alterius – Expression of one thing is the exclusion of another – Where a court consciously and specifically grants certain reliefs but does not advert to other reliefs or rights, the relief so expressly provided necessarily leads to the implied exclusion of the other reliefs and rights.
