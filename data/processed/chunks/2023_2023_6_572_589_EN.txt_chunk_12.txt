In the present case, although F theatre owners may unilaterally determine the conditions of entry into cinema hall, the condition imposed in this instance is not unfair, unreasonable or unconscionable. 31. The condition of entry is imposed as a direct result of the exercise of the right of cinema owners to carry on a business or trade G under Article 19(1)(g) of the Constitution.
