Digital Supreme Court Reports Judicial Discipline – Maintenance of: Held: If a and/or has attained finality because a judicial remedy is either not available in law or even if available, such remedy has been lost, it is not open for a higher court of law by a judicial fiat either to create a remedy for the party on the losing side to pursue or to grant liberty to him to pursue an otherwise available remedy which by passage of time might have been lost-behind the back of a party who would obviously be se- riously affected if he were compelled to contest the proceedings once again – Such an act of court would be without the authority of law.
