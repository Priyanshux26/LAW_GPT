It was the case on behalf of the defendant that in view of the Tamil Nadu Amendment Act No.29 of 2012 to the Indian Registration Act, under which the instruments of B agreement relating to sale of immovable property of the value of Rs.100/ - and upwards is compulsorily required to be registered, the said unregistered document shall be inadmissible in evidence.
