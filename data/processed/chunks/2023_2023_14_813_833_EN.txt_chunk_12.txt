It is axiomatic that the principle of parity is based on the guarantee of positive equality before law enshrined in Article 14 of the Constitution. However, if any illegality or irregularity has been committed in favour of any individual or a group of individuals, or a wrong has been passed by a judicial forum, others cannot invoke the jurisdiction of the higher or superior court for repeating or multiplying the same irregularity or illegality TARUN KUMAR v. ASSISTANT DIRECTOR DIRECTORATE 831 OF ENFORCEMENT [BELA M. TRIVEDI, J.] or for passing similar wrong . Article 14 is not meant to perpetuate the illegality or irregularity. If there has been a benefi t or advantage conferred on one or a set of people by any authority or by the court, without legal basis or justifi cation, other persons could not claim as a matter of right the benefi t on the basis of such wrong decision. 20.
