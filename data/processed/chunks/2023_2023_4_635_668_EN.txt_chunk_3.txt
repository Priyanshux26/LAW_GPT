The did not purport to be one of dismissal for default or on merits and it cannot be taken to F mean other than what it purported to be. It is in ordinary phraseology; not legal phraseology and it cannot be divested of its ordinary meaning. Its ordinary meaning is that the proceeding was closed and the suit would not count as a pending one. The later description would be redundant if the was one of final G disposal of the suit. The did not purport to be a final disposal of the suit. It merely stopped the proceedings. It did nothing more.
