The respondent instituted contempt proceedings before the High Court of Delhi. He was reinstated in service by an dated 8 March 2021. In the course of the hearing of the contempt proceedings, the Single Judge in an dated 2 June 2023, noted the submission of the respondent that even if the date of implementation of the minor penalty was from 16 October 2018, he would be entitled to all promotions till the rank of IG from 2021 till the date of his retirement on 31 March 2021.
