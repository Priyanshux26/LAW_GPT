On point no.4, it was held that defendants 6 to 7 had failed to discharge the burden that they were bona fide purchasers for value without notice. On point no.5, it was held that defendant no.7 had lost the opportunity to contest and on point no.6, the High Court held that the of the Trial Court did not suffer from any infirmity on any count and, accordingly, proceeded to dismiss the appeal while confirming the of the Trial Court. 17.
