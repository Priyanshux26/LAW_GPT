It was noticed by this Court in Neeraj Kumar Rai (supra) that the 2009 Norms and Standards for Secondary Teacher Education Programme through Open and Distance Learning System leading to B.Ed. did not provide for any minimum percentage of marks in Bachelor’s degree.
