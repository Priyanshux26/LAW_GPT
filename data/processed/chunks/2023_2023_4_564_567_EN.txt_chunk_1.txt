The case on behalf of the appellant that as the original writ petitioners were appointed in the year 2007 and thereafter, there were revision of pay and in fact they E were to get the pay in the basic pay scale, they shall not be entitled to the additional 2% has no substance. On conjoint reading of D.O./s dated 27.09.2006 and 02.06.2008, all those employees subject to fulfilment of the conditions mentioned in D.O./ dated 02.06.2008 shall be entitled to the additional 2% in addition to the existing pay, irrespective whether as on 01.04.2003 they were in service or not. Therefore, the F case on behalf of the appellant(s) that as they were not appointed as on 01.04.2003 and they were appointed subsequently in the year 2007 and, therefore, not entitled to the additional 2% without doing the hard work has no substance.
