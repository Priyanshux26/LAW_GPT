Dismissing the appeal, the Court G HELD: 1. Once NCLT is satisfied that the default has occurred, there is hardly a discretion left with NCLT to refuse admission of the application under Section 7. Thus, even the non- payment of a part of debt when it becomes due and payable will amount to default on the part of a Corporate Debtorò.
