The limited scope of the Court under Section 11(6A) at the pre-referral stage is to examine whether the arbitration agreement, prima facie, exists as referred to under Section 7 of the Act, 1996, which includes only the determination of the following factors : (i) Whether the arbitration agreement is in G writing? (ii) Whether the core contractual ingredients qua the arbitration agreement are fulfilled? (iii) On rare occasions, on a serious note of objection, if any, it may examine whether the subject matter of dispute is arbitrable? [Para 92][420-E-F] H 292 SUPREME COURT REPORTS [2023] 9 S.C.R.
